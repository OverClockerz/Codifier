# 14. Additional Details

This document contains miscellaneous information and answers to frequently asked questions about the "Office-RPG" project.

## Target Audience

- **Primary:** Students in university/college (especially in STEM fields), recent graduates, and individuals enrolled in coding bootcamps. This group is actively looking to bridge the gap between their education and their first job.
- **Secondary:** Junior professionals in their first 1-3 years of a corporate career who are looking to upscale their skills and navigate their workplace more effectively.
- **Tertiary:** HR departments and hiring managers in tech companies looking for innovative tools for candidate assessment and employee training.
